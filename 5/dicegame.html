WELCOME TO THE TWO DICE GAME

Rules :

  1. Each player throws both dice once per turn. You only score if you throw doubles.
  2. Players score five points fro double ones, twos, fours and fives. A double six scores twenty five points,but if you throw a double three your score goes back to zero.
  3. Add your score as you play. The first player to get fifty points wins the game.
